What makes Kenilworth different

Kenilworth has a higher proportion of larger, detached and semi-detached properties than the surrounding towns — often with bigger gardens, more outbuildings, and more complex electrical needs than typical urban housing. We do a lot of:

  • Garden office and outbuilding installs — armoured cable runs, sub-distribution, dedicated circuits for heating/lighting/sockets.
  • EV charger installations with longer cable runs — the larger drives often need 15–25m runs from consumer unit to charger position.
  • Multiple-property landlord works — Kenilworth has steady demand from professionals who own a second property elsewhere.
  • Period renovations — particularly properties near the Castle and around Castle Hill/Castle Green.

Burton Green and the outlying villages

CV8 extends well beyond Kenilworth proper into Burton Green, Bubbenhall, and Stoneleigh. We cover all of these from base — they're typically 15–20 minutes away. Rural properties often have their own quirks: TT earthing arrangements (with earth rod) rather than the urban PME standard, older overhead supplies, and outbuildings with sub-supplies that need careful integration into modern installations.

Most common jobs in Kenilworth

  • Consumer unit upgrades (the older estates around Crackley and Whitemoor are mostly due).
  • EV chargers — Kenilworth has high EV uptake.
  • External lighting and security — driveway lights, soffit lights, recessed deck lights.
  • Garden room/office electrical fit-outs.
  • EICRs for HMO and student lets near Kenilworth station.
