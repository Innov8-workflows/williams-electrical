The kind of commercial work we do

We're not a national contractor — we work with small and medium businesses across Warwickshire who want a direct relationship with their electrician. Typical jobs:

Office fit-outs and refurbishments

From single-floor refurbs to whole-building re-services. Cat A and Cat B work — floor boxes, perimeter dado trunking, IT-grade twisted-pair where required, LED panel lighting with daylight/PIR sensors, emergency lighting test circuits. We'll work alongside your IT contractor and project manager.

Retail and hospitality

Shop fit-outs, café and restaurant upgrades, salon installs. Particular attention to point-of-sale power, display lighting (track + spotlights, LED strip, neon-flex feature lighting), and food-prep area compliance (EICR, IP rating, RCD protection).

Light industrial and trade units

Three-phase circuits for machinery, three-phase distribution boards, motor isolators, dust-rated lighting for workshops. We don't do heavy industrial (high-voltage, large motor controls) — but everything up to a typical SME workshop unit, yes.

EICRs and periodic inspections

5-year EICRs on commercial premises are required for insurance and lease compliance. We carry out full electrical inspections out-of-hours where needed (evenings, weekends) so trading isn't interrupted. Reports issued the same week with itemised remedials.

Emergency lighting

BS 5266 compliant emergency lighting design, install, and 6-monthly testing. Test records logged and shared so you've got the paperwork ready for any insurance or fire-officer visit.

Landlord works

Commercial property owners — pre-let EICRs, common-area lighting, fire alarm coordination, mains supply upgrades when tenants change use class.

How we work with businesses

  • Quotation in writing, fixed where the scope allows.
  • 30-day payment terms on commercial accounts (rather than upfront).
  • Out-of-hours scheduling available for retail and hospitality.
  • One point of contact — you call Alfie directly, not a switchboard.
  • £5m public liability insurance. Certificates available on request.
  • NICEIC commercial registration — all work certified and notified properly.
