Where we fit into a solar install

We're not a full-stack solar company — we don't sell roofing or do the structural mounts. What we do, and do properly, is the electrical side of any solar PV or battery installation:

  • DC isolators on the roof side, properly sized and mounted.
  • String wiring routed cleanly and protected against UV degradation.
  • Inverter mounting, AC connection, and final commissioning.
  • Generation meter installation.
  • G98/G99 DNO notification (the electrical paperwork that has to go to your network operator).
  • Battery system DC and AC interconnects (Tesla Powerwall, GivEnergy, Sunsynk, Solis, etc.).
  • Consumer unit modifications — adding generation breakers, dedicated battery circuits.
  • Smart EV charger integration so excess solar diverts to the car (Zappi, Hypervolt with sense).

If you're working with a solar installer who's missing electrical capacity, we can step in as the electrical subcontractor. If you're DIY-ing or part-DIY-ing your install, we'll do just the certifiable electrical work — that's perfectly legal as long as the install is properly notified.

Battery retrofit to existing solar

A lot of our solar work right now is adding battery storage to homes that already have PV. The economics changed dramatically in 2023–2024:

  • SEG export rates rose — most suppliers now pay 6–15p/kWh.
  • Battery prices halved for entry-level systems.
  • Octopus Intelligent Flux and Cosy Octopus introduced cheap overnight charging + export at peak — turning a battery into a near-immediate payback proposition.

If you already have solar and you're feeding most of your generation back to the grid for 5p, you're leaving money on the table. We'll size a battery to your usage pattern (typical 5–10 kWh for a 4 kW system), spec the right model, install, and integrate with your existing PV.

Brands we work with

Most home batteries fall into one of two categories: integrated (battery + inverter in one) like GivEnergy AIO and Tesla Powerwall, or modular (battery + separate inverter) like Pylontech with Sunsynk or Solis. We have no allegiance — we'll spec what fits your existing setup and your priorities (price, app quality, warranty, expandability).

What about EV charger + solar?

If you have solar and you're getting an EV, get a charger that talks to the inverter properly. MyEnergi Zappi is the go-to for solar diversion. Hypervolt 3.0 with the sense module also works well. We install both, configure the solar-diversion modes, and walk you through the app.
